Green Square Packaging Futures

This was a project to explore future circular economy solutions to fast moving consumer goods (FMCG) packaging waste.

*This was a group project in collaboration with Oliver Veal who did the video voiceover, and Kerim Taşkın who was in charge of the slide formatting.

My Work on the Project

Together with Oliver and Kerim, I researched FMCG packaging and its current state. I was in charge of researching the methods of recycling used today, and what systems are in place for them. I was also in charge of animations and most of the graphics in the video.
