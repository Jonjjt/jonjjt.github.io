Electrochromic Day Shapes

The brief for this project was to come up with a solution for hoisting day shapes on an unmanned ship in the best way possible. This method ideally would have a small footprint, be simple and easy to maintain, and able to be remotely controlled.

*This was a one day project that came joint first in a Makeathon organised by the Design Society at Imperial College London.

*This was done in collaboration with Harvey Wong, Andy Ferdinand, Xinyu Bai, and Brandon Tan.

My Work on the Project

Ideation was performed as a group, and I was in charge of FEA, CAD and the still renders.
